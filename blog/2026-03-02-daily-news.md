### Day Three of U.S.-Iran War: Four American Troops Killed, Friendly Fire Incident in Kuwait

![Middle East conflict](/img/blog/2026-03-02/iran-conflict.jpg)

The U.S.-Israeli military campaign against Iran entered its third day with escalating casualties on all sides. The Pentagon confirmed **4 U.S. service members killed** and 4 others seriously wounded during Operation Epic Fury, after an incoming munition struck a tactical operations center in Kuwait. In a separate incident, **three U.S. Air Force fighter jets were mistakenly shot down by Kuwaiti air defenses** — CENTCOM confirmed all six aircrew ejected safely and were recovered in stable condition. Iran's Red Crescent reported **555 people killed** in U.S.-Israeli strikes since the war began Saturday, including a devastating **148 deaths in the Minab school airstrike**. President Trump vowed to "avenge" the deaths of American service members and said major combat operations continue.

### Iran's Retaliatory Strikes Hit Gulf States — Explosions Across Qatar, UAE, Kuwait

Iranian missiles and drones continued striking targets across the Middle East, with explosions reported in **Qatar, the UAE, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, and Jordan**. Loud bangs and sirens were heard in Kuwait City early Monday morning, with smoke rising from near the U.S. Embassy. Kuwaiti air defenses intercepted the majority of drones near the Rumaithiya and Salwa neighborhoods. At least **1 person was killed in Kuwait, 3 in the UAE, and 16 injured in Qatar**. Iran has also struck two vessels traveling through the **Strait of Hormuz**, raising fears of a full oil export blockade. Another **1,579 flights** in and out of major Middle East airports were canceled Sunday, with Dubai International Airport losing 70% of its flights.

### Israel Strikes Beirut After Hezbollah Enters the Fray — 31 Killed in Lebanon

Hezbollah entered the war by launching "a barrage of precision missiles and a swarm of drones" at the **Mishmar al-Karmel missile defense facility** south of Haifa, Israel. In response, Israeli jets bombed **Beirut's southern suburbs**, killing at least **31 people and injuring 149**, according to Lebanon's Health Ministry. Israel ordered evacuations in **more than 50 villages** across southern Lebanon and the Bekaa Valley, sending masses of displaced civilians fleeing toward Beirut. In a dramatic move, Lebanese Prime Minister **Nawaf Salam** condemned Hezbollah's strikes as illegal and announced a **total ban on all military activities by Hezbollah**, demanding the group surrender its weapons to the state.

### 22 Killed as Protesters Storm U.S. Consulate in Karachi, Pakistan

Violent protests erupted in Pakistan following the killing of Iran's Supreme Leader Khamenei, with demonstrators storming the **U.S. Consulate in Karachi**. At least **22 people were killed and over 120 injured** as security forces responded with batons, tear gas, and gunfire. Protesters set fires and smashed windows at the consulate on Mai Kolachi Road. The U.S. Embassy in Islamabad issued a security alert and **shut down all diplomatic facilities across Pakistan**, canceling all appointments scheduled for March 2. Consulates in Karachi, Lahore, and Peshawar were closed.

Sources: [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/2026/03/01/pakistan-protesters-attacked-us-consulate-karachi-iran/106eebe8-1545-11f1-8e8d-fe91db44677b_story.html) · [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/3/1/at-least-9-killed-in-pro-iran-protest-at-us-consulate-in-pakistans-karachi) · [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-01/nine-killed-as-protesters-try-to-storm-us-consulate-in-pakistan)

### Global Markets in Turmoil — Oil Surges 9%, Stocks Plunge Worldwide

![Stock market volatility](/img/blog/2026-03-02/stock-market.jpg)

### SpaceX Doubles Up — 54 Starlink Satellites Deployed in Two Sunday Launches

![SpaceX Falcon 9 launch](/img/blog/2026-03-02/spacex-launch.jpg)

SpaceX pulled off two Falcon 9 launches on Sunday, deploying a total of **54 Starlink satellites** from both coasts. From **Cape Canaveral**, a Falcon 9 launched **29 Starlink satellites** at 9:56 PM ET — the booster was making its remarkable **26th flight**, having previously supported Crew-6 and 20 other Starlink missions. SpaceX has now launched over **500 Starlink satellites** in 2026 alone, maintaining its relentless deployment cadence.

### White House Tech Summit on March 4 — "Rate Payer Protection Pledge"

The Trump administration is hosting **Amazon, Google, Meta, Microsoft, xAI, Oracle, and OpenAI** at the White House on Wednesday to sign the **"Rate Payer Protection Pledge"** — requiring tech giants to build, buy, or bring their own power for AI data centers rather than passing electricity costs to consumers. The initiative responds to surging power demand from AI infrastructure. Notably, **Anthropic** has already committed to covering 100% of consumer electricity price increases from its data centers — one of the first AI companies to make such a pledge — despite its ongoing dispute with the administration over military AI safeguards.

Sources: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/25/trump-tech-ai-data-center-electricity-price-pledge.html) · [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/02/25/trump-data-center-electricity-ratepayer-protection-pledge) · [Tom's Hardware](https://www.tomshardware.com/tech-industry/big-tech/trump-orders-big-tech-to-generate-its-own-power-for-ai-data-centers-reveals-new-ratepayer-protection-pledge-to-curb-rising-electricity-prices-in-the-u-s)

## Research

### NVIDIA Drops New Physical AI Models — GR00T N1.6 and Cosmos 2.5 for Robotics

![AI and robotics research](/img/blog/2026-03-02/robotics-ai.jpg)

Ahead of GTC 2026 (March 16–19 in San Jose), NVIDIA released a wave of open-source physical AI models. **Isaac GR00T N1.6** is an open reasoning vision-language-action (VLA) model purpose-built for humanoid robots, enabling full-body control with **Cosmos Reason** for contextual understanding. **Cosmos Predict 2.5** generates physically-based synthetic data for robot policy evaluation, while **Cosmos Reason 2** is a vision-language model that lets machines "see, understand and act in the physical world." Industry leaders including **Boston Dynamics, Caterpillar, Franka Robotics, LG Electronics, and NEURA Robotics** are using the stack to debut next-generation robots. **Isaac Lab-Arena**, a new open-source framework, standardizes robot policy benchmarking with connections to Libero and Robocasa.

### Physical AI Hits Inflection Point — From Labs to Factory Floors

The convergence of AI and robotics is reaching an inflection point in 2026. **Boston Dynamics** demonstrated its fully electric Atlas performing complex autonomous tasks in factory settings using reinforcement learning — adapting to shifting environments rather than following rigid programming. **UBTech** has now produced **1,000 Walker S2 humanoid robots** at its Liuzhou factory, with over 500 deployed to **BYD, Geely, Foxconn, and SF Express**, targeting 5,000 units annually. The **International Federation of Robotics** identified agentic AI as a top-5 global robotics trend, with **3 in 4 companies** planning to deploy agentic AI within two years. The industry is shifting from proof-of-concept demos to commercial-scale deployment.

### Figure CEO Predicts 2026 Breakthroughs: Humanoids, eVTOLs, and Memory AI

**Figure** CEO Brett Adcock predicts 2026 will be the year science fiction becomes reality across three domains: **humanoid robots will work autonomously** in commercial settings, electric vertical takeoff and landing aircraft (**eVTOLs**) will undergo city tests, and AI will shift to **multimodal memory agents** capable of retaining and reasoning across extended interactions. The prediction aligns with broader industry momentum — agentic AI workloads are now generating measurable revenue at NVIDIA's hyperscale customers, and humanoid robotics companies are moving from R&D budgets to manufacturing lines. Schools are also beta-testing **20-foot standoff weapon detection systems**, another AI-physical crossover application gaining real-world traction.
