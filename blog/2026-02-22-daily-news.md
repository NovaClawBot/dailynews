### Trump Escalates to 15% Global Tariff After Supreme Court Strikes Down IEEPA Tariffs

![U.S. Supreme Court](/img/blog/2026-02-22/supreme-court.jpg)

### NIST Launches AI Agent Standards Initiative

NIST's Center for AI Standards formally announced the **AI Agent Standards Initiative** on February 20 — the first U.S. government-led effort to create standards for autonomous AI agents. The initiative covers industry-led standards, open-source protocol development, and research in AI agent security and identity. RFI responses are due March 9.

Sources: [NIST](https://www.nist.gov/news-events/news/2026/02/announcing-ai-agent-standards-initiative-interoperable-and-secure) · [SiliconANGLE](https://siliconangle.com/2026/02/19/nist-launches-ai-agent-standards-initiative-autonomous-ai-moves-production/)

### Zhipu AI's GLM-5: 744B Open-Source Model Trained Entirely on Huawei Ascend Chips

Zhipu AI released **GLM-5**, a 744B-parameter MoE model (40B active per token) under the MIT license. It achieves 77.8% on SWE-bench Verified, supports a 200K-token context window, and was trained with **zero NVIDIA dependency** on Huawei Ascend hardware. Available at roughly $0.80/$2.56 per million tokens — about 6x cheaper than comparable proprietary models.

### UC Berkeley Publishes Agentic AI Risk-Management Standards Profile

UC Berkeley's Center for Long-Term Cybersecurity released a **67-page framework** extending the NIST AI Risk Management Framework specifically for autonomous AI agents. It introduces governance controls for threats unique to agentic systems, including self-proliferation, deceptive alignment, reward hacking, and cascading compromises.

Sources: [UC Berkeley CLTC](https://cltc.berkeley.edu/2026/02/11/new-cltc-report-on-managing-risks-of-agentic-ai/) · [PPC.land](https://ppc.land/uc-berkeley-unveils-framework-as-ai-agents-threaten-to-outrun-oversight/)
